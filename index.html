About Night Watch Companions

Dedicated to Breeding Exceptional Dobermanns

With over 15 years of experience, Night Watch Companions has established itself as a premier Dobermann breeding facility. Our commitment to excellence in breeding, health, and temperament has made us a trusted name among Dobermann enthusiasts.

We specialize in European working line Dobermanns, known for their intelligence, loyalty, and protective instincts. Each puppy is carefully bred to meet the highest standards of the breed while maintaining the gentle family companion temperament that makes Dobermans exceptional pets.

Our breeding program focuses on health, temperament, and conformation. All our breeding dogs undergo extensive health testing including hip/elbow evaluations, cardiac screening, and genetic testing.
